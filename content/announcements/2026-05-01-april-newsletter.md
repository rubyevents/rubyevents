April was a busy month month for the Ruby community with 5 conferences - Tropical on Rails, wroclove.rb, RubyKaigi, Haggis Ruby, and Blue Ridge Ruby! May will be another busy month with Rubycon, Balkan Ruby, RubyConf Austria, and Sekigahara RubyKaigi! We merged 40 PRs from 22 contributors this month.

## Open CFPs

- The [RubyConf](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/rubyconf-2026/cfp) Community Day [Application](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeFyvgo05RYHsI-UPylYYq3KTgfgPPAE1pVZKhbVGXZKrIKFw/viewform) closes **TODAY** (May 1)! The conference will be in Las Vegas, NV from July 14-16.
- [Rails at Scale Summit](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/rails-at-scale-summit-2026/cfp) is accepting [applications](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdNzzOKAWl4VdF8uLmx_Rdm4ElL0x5Vqg6sPKWNlFq711SDYg/viewform) for speakers and attendees and closes soon on May 8. The summit will be in Austin, TX on September 22, the day before RailsWorld.
- The [Ruby Taiwan Track @ COSCUP 2026](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/coscup-2026/cfp) [CFP](https://pretalx.coscup.org/coscup-2026/cfp) closes May 9. The conference will be in Taipei, Taiwan on August 8-9.
- [Kansai RubyKaigi 09](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/kansai-rubykaigi-09/cfp) will be in Otsu, Shiga, Japan on July 18. The [CFP](https://forms.gle/hRjJabKiLXH5DreZ8) closes May 10.
- [Rails World](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/rails-world-2026/cfp)'s [CFP](https://sessionize.com/rails-world-2026/) closes May 16. The conference will be in Austin, TX on September 23-24.
- [Deccan Queen on Rails](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/deccan-queen-on-rails-2026/cfp) will be in Pune, Maharashtra, India from October 8-9. The [CFP](https://cfp.deccanqueenonrails.com/) closes May 31.
- [tiny ruby #{conf}](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/tiny-ruby-conf-2026/cfp) will be in Helsinki, Finland on October 1. The [CFP](https://cfp.helsinkiruby.fi) closes June 14.

> None of these feel quite right? Check out our list of [meetup cfps](https://www.rubyevents.org/cfp?kind=meetup)! Many are open year round and always looking for new talks.

## This month's events

- [Tropical on Rails](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/tropical-on-rails-2026) was in São Paulo, Brazil on April 9-10.
- [Rails Girls São Paulo Evolution](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/rails-girls-sao-paulo-evolution-2026) was a workshop held April 11,2026 immediately following Tropical on Rails.
- [wroclove.rb](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/wroclove-rb-2026) was in Wrocław, Poland from April 17-19.
- [RubyKaigi](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/rubykaigi-2026) was in Hakodate, Hokkaido, Japan from April 22-24.
- [Haggis Ruby](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/haggis-ruby-2026) was in Glasgow, Scotland from April 23-24.
- [Blue Ridge Ruby](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/blue-ridge-ruby-2026) was in Asheville, NC from April 30-May 1.

> Did you attend a Ruby event this month that isn't on the list? Let us know with an [issue](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/issues/new) or [PR](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)!

## Next month's events

- [Rubycon](https://rubyevents.org/events/rubycon-2026) will be in Rimini, Italy on May 8. [Tickets](https://ti.to/rubycon/rubycon-2026) are available for €150. They're having a toga party at the end of the day, so don't forget your sheets! _Say hi to RubyEvents team member, Marco Roth!_
- [Balkan Ruby](https://rubyevents.org/events/balkanruby-2026) will be in Sofia, Bulgaria from May 15-16. [Tickets](https://balkanruby.com/checkouts) are available for €120-200, or you can buy a single-day ticket for €70.
- [RubyConf Austria](https://rubyevents.org/events/rubyconf-austria-2026) will be in Vienna, Austria from May 29-31 at the Das MuTh theater. [Tickets](https://ti.to/rubyconfat/2026) are available for €200!
- [Sekigahara RubyKaigi 01](https://rubyevents.org/events/sekigahara-rubykaigi-01) will be in Sekigahara, Gifu, Japan on May 30. [Tickets](https://ti.to/sekigahara/01) are avilable for ¥1,500-¥5,000.

> Prepare more than a month ahead by checking out our [calendar](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/2026) or the full [list of events](https://www.rubyevents.org/events).
