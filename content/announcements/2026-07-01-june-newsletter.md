## Open CFPs

- [Kaigi on Rails 2026](/events/kaigi-on-rails-2026/cfp) will be in Shibuya, Tokyo, Japan from October 16-17. The [CFP](https://cfp.kaigionrails.org/events/2026/) closes July 12.
- [Helvetic Ruby 2026](/events/helveticruby-2026/cfp) will be in Zurich, Switzerland from November 19-20. The [CFP](https://helvetic-ruby.ch/call-for-speakers/) closes July 31.

> None of these feel quite right? Check out our list of [meetup cfps](https://www.rubyevents.org/cfp?kind=meetup)! Many are open year round and always looking for new talks.

## June's events

- [Matsue RubyKaigi 12](/events/matsue-rubykaigi-12) was in Matsue, Japan on June 06. 
- [Blastoff Rails](/events/blastoffrails-2026) was in Albuquerque, NM, United States from June 11-12. [Videos are available!](/events/blastoffrails-2026/videos)
- [Brighton Ruby](/events/brightonruby-2026) was in Brighton, UK on June 25. 

> Did you attend a Ruby event this month that isn't on the list? Let us know with an [issue](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/issues/new) or [PR](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)!

## July's events

- [Ruby Retreat 2026](/events/ruby-retreat-au-2026) will be in Sydney, NSW, AU from July 03-06. [Tickets](https://ti.to/ruby-australia/ruby-retreat-2026) are no longer available.
- [RubyConf 2026](/events/rubyconf-2026) will be in Las Vegas, NV, United States from July 14-16. [Tickets](https://ti.to/rubyconf/rubyconf-2026) are available for $995 and $375 for students.
- [Kansai RubyKaigi 09](/events/kansai-rubykaigi-09) will be in Otsu, Japan on July 18. Registration is required, but [tickets](https://ti.to/kansairubykaigi/09) are free!

> Prepare more than a few months ahead by checking out our [calendar](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/2026) or the full [list of events](https://www.rubyevents.org/events).
