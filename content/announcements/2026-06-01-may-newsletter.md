In May, we had 5 conferences - Blue Ridge Ruby, Rubycon, Balkan Ruby, RubyConf Austria, and Sekigahara RubyKaigi! June will have Matsue RubyKaigi, Blastoff Rails, and Brighton Ruby! Unfortunately, Baltic Ruby has been cancelled. We merged 54 PRs from 21 contributors this month!

## Open CFPs

- [tiny ruby #{conf}](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/tiny-ruby-conf-2026/cfp) will be in Helsinki, Finland on October 1. The [CFP](https://cfp.helsinkiruby.fi) closes June 14.
- [EuRuKo](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/euruko-2026/cfp) will be in Brno, Czechia on September 16-18. The [CFP](https://cfp2026.euruko.org/events/euruko-2026) closes June 16.
- [Rocky Mountain Ruby](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/rocky-mountain-ruby-2026/cfp) will be in Boulder, CO, United States on September 28-29. The [CFP](https://sessionize.com/rocky-mountain-ruby-2026/) closes June 30.
- XO Ruby is back with 3 new locations - [Salt Lake City](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/xoruby-salt-lake-city-2026/cfp) (Aug 1), [Seattle](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/xoruby-seattle-2026/cfp) (Aug 7), and [Vancouver](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/xoruby-vancouver-2026/cfp) (Aug 15)! All 3 events use the same [CFP](https://www.xoruby.com/speak/) which closes June 30.

> None of these feel quite right? Check out our list of [meetup cfps](https://www.rubyevents.org/cfp?kind=meetup)! Many are open year round and always looking for new talks.

## This month's events

- [Blue Ridge Ruby](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/blue-ridge-ruby-2026) was in Asheville, NC from April 30-May 1. [Videos](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/blue-ridge-ruby-2026/talks) are already released!
- [Rubycon](https://rubyevents.org/events/rubycon-2026) was in Rimini, Italy on June 8.
- [Balkan Ruby](https://rubyevents.org/events/balkanruby-2026) was in Sofia, Bulgaria from May 15-16.
- [RubyConf Austria](https://rubyevents.org/events/rubyconf-austria-2026) was in Vienna, Austria from May 29-31 at the Das MuTh theater.
- [Sekigahara RubyKaigi 01](https://rubyevents.org/events/sekigahara-rubykaigi-01) was in Sekigahara, Gifu, Japan on May 30.

> Did you attend a Ruby event this month that isn't on the list? Let us know with an [issue](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/issues/new) or [PR](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md)!

## Next month's events

- [Matsue RubyKaigi 12](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/matsue-rubykaigi-12) will be in Matsue, Japan on June 6. [Tickets](https://matsue-rb.doorkeeper.jp/events/196467) appear to be sold out - check the site for any updates!
- [Blastoff Rails](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/blastoff-rails-2026) will be in Albuquerque, NM from June 11-12. [Tickets](https://ti.to/blastoff-rails/2026) are $299 (or $399 for supporters). Play the [Blastoff Rails game](https://game.blastoffrails.com) while you're waiting for the event!
- [Brighton Ruby](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/brightonruby-2026) will be in Brighton, England on June 25. Only "latest" tier [tickets](https://ti.to/goodscary/brightonruby-2026) are still available for £179.00.
- _If you were planning on attending Baltic Ruby, please be advised that it has been cancelled._

> Prepare more than a few months ahead by checking out our [calendar](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/2026) or the full [list of events](https://www.rubyevents.org/events).

## Contributions

This month, we merged [54 PRs from 21 contributors!](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/pulls?q=is%3Apr+is%3Amerged+created%3A>%3D2026-05-01+-created%3A>2026-06-01+) 26 PRs were [new event data and videos](https://github.com/rubyevents/rubyevents/pulls?q=is%3Apr+is%3Amerged+created%3A>%3D2026-05-01+-created%3A>2026-06-01+label%3Acontent).

### Big Updates

On the [calendar view](https://www.rubyevents.org/events/2026) of the events page, you'll notice a new "Subscribe to calendar" which is an ics feed of all events on RubyEvents.org. You can add this to your calendar app of choice to stay up to date on all the events in the Ruby community! Thanks, @dennispaagman!
